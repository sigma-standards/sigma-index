## Glossary

**Phase:** Major development cycle (0-9, 10 phases total)
**Domain:** Category of standards (40+ canonical domains)
**Meta-Layer:** Grouping of domains (6 layers: Life Sciences, Physical Sciences, Society & Governance, Economy & Trade, Technology & Infrastructure, Environment)
**Entry:** Individual standard/framework/treaty/guideline/recommendation (target 250K+)
**Edge:** Relationship between entries (target 50K+)
**Sigma_id:** Unique identifier per entry (format: DOMAIN_CODE-ISSUER_CODE-STD_NUMBER-YEAR)
**MVP:** Minimum Viable Product (current: 88,203 entries at 33% completion)
**v1.0:** Final production release (target: 250,000+ entries at 100% completion)
